## Graphic Design Specifications

### Instagram Post Specifications

**Dimensions**: 1080 x 1080 pixels (square) or 1080 x 1350 pixels (portrait)
**Format**: PNG or JPG (PNG preferred for transparency)
**Resolution**: 72 DPI minimum
**Color Mode**: RGB
**File Size**: Under 8 MB

**Design Elements**:
- Branded border or corner elements
- Consistent typography
- Hierarchy with title, content, CTA
- White space for readability
- Brand colors (Primary: #2D3748, Accent: #48BB78, Secondary: #667EEA)

### Instagram Story Specifications

**Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 pixels
**Format**: PNG or JPG
**Duration**: 5-15 seconds for text, 15-30 seconds for video
**Safe Area**: Keep important content within center 400 pixels wide

**Story Elements**:
- Sticker locations (top-right for handles)
- Text readability against background
- Call-to-action buttons (swipe-up when verified)
- Brand consistency

### Twitter/X Post Graphics

**Optimal Image Size**: 1200 x 675 pixels
**Format**: JPG or PNG
**Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
**File Size**: Under 5 MB
**Resolution**: 72 DPI

**Design Tips**:
- Text should be readable at thumbnail size
- Leave margins for Twitter's image cropping
- High contrast for visibility in feeds
- Avoid busy backgrounds

### Code Snippet Styling

**Font**: JetBrains Mono, 14-16pt for Instagram; 12-14pt for Twitter
**Background**: Dark (#1A202C)
**Text Color**: Light (#E2E8F0)
**Syntax Highlights**:
- Keywords: #48BB78 (green)
- Strings: #F6AD55 (orange)
- Numbers: #667EEA (purple)
- Comments: #718096 (gray)
